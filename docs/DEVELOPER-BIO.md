# Developer Biography

**Aarav Raj** -- Full Stack Developer

Aarav is a full-stack developer with deep experience in React Native, Expo, TypeScript, and Web3 technologies. He built MumMoney as a solo hackathon project driven by the belief that practical financial tools should be accessible to everyday people, not just those with finance backgrounds. His technical breadth spans mobile and web development, backend services with Supabase, subscription management with RevenueCat, and AI integration, all of which come together in MumMoney to help busy mums take control of their money.
